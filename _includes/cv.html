    APE: Automated Print Extractor

    Our first project is the APE. We are proud to announce that our robot can harvest, extract, and transport all 3D printed items in any FDM 3D Printing Farm. This means that the APE can remove and replace the bedsheet off the 3D printer, extract the 3D printed part off the bedsheet, aand transport it to any predetermined location in the farm. It is the first step to having a completely automated 3D printing factory..
